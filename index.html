Tableau Dashboard

PL/SQL
Project

In this project, I meticulously crafted SQL code to establish a robust database framework tailored for an online bicycle rental enterprise. Through the creation of multiple tables and the seamless integration of relevant data, I orchestrated a comprehensive repository conducive to efficient operations. Moreover, I leveraged Procedural Language (PL) to implement two pivotal triggers designed to enforce essential business protocols. The first trigger meticulously regulates customer transactions, ensuring that no more than two bicycles are rented simultaneously, thus optimizing resource allocation and maintaining service quality. Meanwhile, the second trigger serves as a safeguard against potential financial risks, swiftly halting bike rentals for users with outstanding balances exceeding $500, thereby safeguarding the company's fiscal integrity and ensuring seamless transactions.
